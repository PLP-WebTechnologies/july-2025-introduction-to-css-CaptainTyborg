About Football

Football is the world’s most popular sport, played by millions and loved by billions. Just like football has rules, CSS has rules to style and organize web pages.

The Box Model in Action

Think of each player on the pitch as a "box":

  • Content: The player’s skills and actions
  • Padding: Space around the player within their role
  • Border: The jersey and outline that defines them
  • Margin: The distance between them and other players
